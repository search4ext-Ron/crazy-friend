# Finding "New Project" in Railway

The button might be in different places depending on your Railway dashboard view.

## Where to Look:

### Option 1: Top Right Corner
- Look for a **"+"** button (plus icon)
- Or **"New"** button
- Or **"Create"** button
- Usually in the top navigation bar

### Option 2: Dashboard Home
- If you're on the Railway home/dashboard
- Look for a large **"New Project"** card or button
- Might say **"Create Project"** or **"Start New Project"**

### Option 3: Left Sidebar
- Check the left sidebar menu
- Look for **"Projects"** â†’ then a **"+"** or **"New"** button

### Option 4: Empty State
- If you have no projects yet, there might be a big **"Create Your First Project"** button in the center

### Option 5: Projects Page
- Click on **"Projects"** in the left menu (if visible)
- Then look for **"New Project"** or **"+"** button
